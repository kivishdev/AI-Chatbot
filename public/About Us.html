About AI Chatbot

Welcome to our AI Chatbot! This project leverages cutting-edge technology to provide intelligent responses based on user inputs. The chatbot is built using Gemini AI, a powerful generative language model developed by Google that is designed to understand and generate human-like responses.

What is Gemini AI?

Gemini AI is a generative language model developed by Google, known for its advanced capabilities in natural language understanding and generation. Built on the latest advancements in machine learning, Gemini AI offers a versatile, conversational AI that can understand prompts and generate human-like responses. It is designed to be highly efficient, capable of answering a wide variety of queries, and adapting to a wide range of applications, including chatbots, content generation, and more.
